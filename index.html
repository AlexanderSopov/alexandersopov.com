LäsTrafik - Android Application




This is a project some classmates and I built in University. It's an Android application that allows the user to read and listen to books while riding in public transportation.

The Idea is based on the free-to-play model where you can listen or read to books for free while on the bus or tram, but if you want to continue, then you'll need to pay for it.

Gin & Juicy - Hip Hop Club




A friend and I didn't like the lack of Hip Hop music in Gothenburg's club scene, so we decided to fix it. We hired Dj's, artists, made deals with different venues and planned events. Slowly but surely the genre grew. Our proudest moment was when Metro Newspaper put us on the front page!
